#### What is Tap & Pay?


 


![](/assets/images/posts/nfc.jpg)


When we use a [Contactless Card](/2014/05/11/mobile-payments-what-is-a-contactless-chip-card.html) to tap on the Contactless reader to make a payment, instead of swiping a [magstripe](/2014/04/10/mobile-payments-what-is-a-magnetic-stripe-card.html) card, we are essentially using Tap & Pay technology. The same goes for any NFC enabled mobile device that support `Card Emulation Mode` – like Android and Blackberry. Tap & Pay is just a marketing terminology. You may also see it referred to as `Tap-n-Pay`, `Tap to Pay`, `Tap & Go`, `Wave & Pay` and so on.
