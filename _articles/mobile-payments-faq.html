Mobile Payments FAQ

A comprehensive series on the world of mobile payments — how it works, who the players are, and what the key technologies look like.

This series started as a Mobile payments FAQ and not so FAQ — an attempt to document and share things I learned while working in the mobile payments industry. The goal is to make this complex domain more approachable for developers, product folks, and the technically curious.
